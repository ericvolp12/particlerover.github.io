A little about me...
Personal History:
I grew up mainly in the Philadelphia area, but my family currently lives in London, UK. I was a visual art student for several years while in secondary school with the Philadelphia Academy of Fine Arts, and the Moore College of Art and Design in Philadelphia, but I ceased pursuing an artistic career when I decided to focus on my interest in physics before leaving for university. I moved to Chicago in 2013, where I completed my undergraduate degree in physics with a specialization in astrophysics at the University of Chicago (class of 2017). I took a year after graduation to focus on research, and have been working as a full-time researcher for the astrophysics departments of O of C and Northwestern University since my graduation.
